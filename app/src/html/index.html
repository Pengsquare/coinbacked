A Solana token protocol to put the trust where the coin is.

⭣

01/ Why

The crypto space has been plagued by a trust problem due to the lack of stability and extreme volatility of digital currencies. While many people have invested with the hope of making a profit, the unpredictable nature of the market has caused significant anxiety and frustration for investors. During the current bear market, prices for cryptos and tokens can fall by significant percentages, and investors may experience losses on their investments. The huge lack of trust is hurting creators, collectors, and the whole ecosystem.

02/ What

An artistic statue made of silver may have intrinsic value as both a work of art and a precious metal. Its intrinsic value is a combination of both its aesthetic and material qualities. As a work of art, the status maybe admired for its aesthetic qualities. The value of the statue may fluctuate over time, but its status as a unique and valuable object is likely to endure. Why not follow this concept of „intrinsic“ value and apply it to any token, fungible or non-fungible?

03/ How

With Coinbacked, a tansparent protocol is introduced to add intrinsict value to any fungible or non-fungible token by backing it with native SOL. Backing itself is a one-way operation, that can only add value. The only way to retrieve the value is to burn the token. As with the statue made of sivler, the intrinsic value can be only accessed by destroying the work itself.

01/Create & Back

Add intrinsic value to any Solana fungible or non-fungible token in our app or on supported marketplaces. You decide how much SOL you put up for backing! The protocol will take small fee.

02/It's still you token

Coinbacked is a fully transparent protocol. You can do whatever you want to do with your token, Coinbacked is like and add-on or decoration: your NFT just stays the same!

03/Burn it!

The only way to get the backing SOL is to burn the NFT (or amount of fungible tokens). So you bought some shitty NFT, but it was backed? Well, just burn it an retrieve the intrinsic value.

For Token Owners & Collectors

Token onwners and collectors profit from the simple mechanism to safeguards their investments. A backed token builds stability and trust. The backing amount can always be increased, but never be decreased. Long-term commitments replacing the short-term gain - that's Coinbacked!

For Creators & Developers

Add intrinsic value to your work! Show your fans and customers how much you value your art and tokens buy backing them from the day one! Any Solana token your create (fungible or non-fungible) can be backed. Stability and trust now have a protocol: Coinbacked.

For Marketplaces & Exchanges

Add native Coinbacked support to your marketplace and help your customers to build trust. By adding protocol support, marketplaces are an important piece of the trust-puzzle. With Coinbacked in place, a longlasting and sustainable token economy is easy to build.
