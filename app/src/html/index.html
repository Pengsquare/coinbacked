01/ Why

Problem with trust and stabilty of value, volatile to the extreme of chaos and Solution is adding a backing mechanism that locks in native coin of a respective chain tighly associtated and not removable with token itself. Take a beatiful sculputre made of silver: any potential collector would aquire such a piece for the artistic value, but would probably also figure in the fact, that the base price of this scultpule will never fall under the value of its material - here it's the value silver. This right there introduces a very basic level of trust: by choosing a valued material, the creatore deemed the sculpture of enoguh value, that she invested herself by using valuable materials that will not demean.
